Watch F-150s roll off the line at Ford's historic Rouge plant. Industrial tourism at its finest.

Tyree Guyton's outdoor art installation transformed a blighted block into a thought-provoking experience.

Sugar House, Bad Luck Bar, and The Skip. Detroit's cocktail scene punches above its weight.

Windsor is a 5-minute tunnel ride away. Different drinking age, different vibe. Bring your passport.

Tigers at Comerica Park, Lions at Ford Field, or Pistons/Red Wings at Little Caesars Arena ‚Äî all walkable downtown.
